Tech Nephew started where most family tech help starts: with my mom asking why her printer was offline at 9pm. Then my aunt. Then her friend. After a few years of these calls, I realized the people I love most weren't getting stuck on hard problems — they were getting stuck because nobody had ever sat with them long enough to explain. So I made the sitting-with-people part the job. I'll meet you exactly where you are. I won't talk over your head, and I won't talk down to you.
